Anton Isopoussu

Email: anton.isopoussu (α) gmail.com


I work as a Senior Applied Reserach Scientist at Unlikely AI. My work focuses on using AI to solve problems where a mere answer is not enough. I primarily explore techniques which can be thought of exploration of program space by thinking of a reasoned output from a machine learning model as an executable program. The methods are based on combining generative and classical AI in creative ways.

Previously, at Invenia Labs I worked on novel forecasting methods for high dimensional, heavy tailed data.

I was a Senior Research Scientist at Nokia Bell Labs Cambridge, led by Fahim Kawsar, where our group worked on Ubiquitous Computing and Human-computer interaction. My own work mainly focused on machine learning under dataset shift.

I worked as a Senior Researcher as part of the Advanced Research Labs at Nokia Technologies, where I worked on various applications of machine learning in image processing, biometrics and sensing.

I did my PhD in mathematics at the University of Cambridge. My specialisation was in algebraic geometry and geometric analysis.
